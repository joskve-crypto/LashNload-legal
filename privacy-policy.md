PRIVACY POLICY – LASHNLOAD
Effective Date: 16 April 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how Kvedos ( "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal data when you use the LashNload mobile application and web portal (the "Service"). This policy is designed to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Norwegian Personal Data Act.
________________________________________
1. DATA CONTROLLER AND PROCESSOR
•	Consumer Users: Kvedos acts as the Data Controller for your personal data.
•	Business Users: Your employer or the organisation you represent acts as the Data Controller. Kvedos acts as a Data Processor on behalf of the organisation. In such cases, our processing is governed by a separate Data Processing Agreement (DPA).
________________________________________
2. DATA WE COLLECT
We process personal data only when necessary to provide the Service.
2.1 Information Provided by You
•	Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and authentication identifiers provided by third-party identity providers (e.g., Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple).
•	Subscription and Billing Data: We process your subscription status and transaction history. Note: All payment card details are handled exclusively by our third-party payment processors (Stripe, Apple, or Google). Kvedos does not store or see your raw payment information.
2.2 Data Processed Locally (Non-Uploaded Data)
To ensure maximum privacy and minimise our data footprint, the following information is processed and stored strictly locally on your device:
•	Calculations: All input variables (cargo mass, friction coefficients, angles) and the resulting estimates.
•	Photographic Evidence: Images of cargo captured via the App's camera.
•	PDF Reports: Generated reports remain on your device until you choose to share them manually.
2.3 Device and Sensor Data
•	Motion Sensors: We access the gyroscope and accelerometer to facilitate angle measurements. This data is processed in real-time and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.
•	Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymised, aggregated data regarding app performance and crashes to improve the Service’s reliability.
________________________________________
3. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING
Under the GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:
•	Contractual Necessity (Art. 6(1)(b)): To manage your account and provide the calculation features you have subscribed to.
•	Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): To ensure the security of the Service and to perform anonymised analytics for product improvement.
•	Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Where processing is required for tax, accounting, or regulatory compliance.
________________________________________
4. DATA SHARING AND THIRD PARTIES
We do not sell your personal data. We share information with third parties only as necessary to operate the Service:
•	Authentication Providers: Google and Apple (for secure sign-in).
•	Payment Processors: Stripe, Apple App Store, and Google Play Store.
•	Infrastructure Providers: We use secure cloud hosting providers located within the European Economic Area (EEA).
________________________________________
5. LOCAL STORAGE AND USER RESPONSIBILITY
As stipulated in our Terms and Conditions, Kvedos does not have access to your locally stored PDF reports or photographs. If you uninstall the App or lose your device, this data cannot be recovered by Kvedos. You are solely responsible for exporting and archiving your documentation to your own secure systems.
________________________________________
6. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
We primarily store data within the EEA. Should any data be transferred to a country outside the EEA (e.g., through a third-party service provider), we ensure that such transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other legally recognised adequacy mechanisms.
________________________________________
7. YOUR RIGHTS
Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:
•	Access: The right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
•	Rectification: The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
•	Erasure: The right to request the deletion of your account and data.
•	Restriction: The right to limit how we process your data.
•	Data Portability: The right to receive your data in a machine-readable format.
To exercise these rights, please use the settings within the App or contact us directly.
________________________________________
8. DATA SECURITY
We implement robust technical and organisational security measures, including encryption of data in transit, to protect your information. However, since the majority of your operational data (reports and images) is stored locally, the security of that data remains your responsibility through the security settings of your mobile device.
